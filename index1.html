About the movie

"Beauty and the Beast" is a classic fairy tale that has been adapted into various forms of media, including books, films, and stage productions. The story typically revolves around a young woman and a cursed prince who has been transformed into a monstrous beast. The tale emphasizes themes of inner beauty, love, and personal growth. The main storyline often includes these key elements: The Curse: A selfish and arrogant prince is cursed by an enchantress and transformed into a beastly creature. The curse can only be broken if he learns to love someone truly and earns their love in return before a magical rose loses all its petals. Belle, the Protagonist: Belle, a kind, intelligent, and independent young woman, lives in a nearby village. She is seen as different due to her love for reading and her unconventional thinking, which sets her apart from others in the town.
